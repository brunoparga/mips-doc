addiu    $8,$0,-86

Answer:

Yes. The immediate operand -86 is sign-extended to 32 bits then added to a 32-bit zero. The sum (-86) is loaded into $8 .


The Absent Subtract Immediate

You would expect that since there are add, addu, addi, addiu and since there are sub, subu that there would be subtract immediate instructions.

But there are no subtract immediate instructions. They are not really needed. Use the add immediate instruction with a negative immediate operand. To subtract 201 from register $10 do this: 


addiu    $8,$10,-201    #  $8 = $10 - 201
