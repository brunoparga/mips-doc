Another Conversion

To convert 247 from decimal to base 16, repeatedly divide by 16, collecting the remainders right to left as the base 16 expression. This is shown above. Check the result by converting the base 16 expression to decimal: 

 
0xF7  =  F × sixteen + 7 × 1 
  =  15 × 16 + 7 
  =  240 + 7 = 24710

The algorithm is:

  • Divide the number by the base
  • The remainder is the digit
  • The quotient becomes the number
  • Repeat until number is zero

The digits come out right to left
