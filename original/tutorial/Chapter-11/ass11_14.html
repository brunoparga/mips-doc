What is the exclusive-OR of a bit pattern with itself?

Answer:

The result is all zero-bits.


End of the Chapter

An exclusive or of a bit pattern with itself results in all zeros. This is frequently used fact.

Because the two operands are identical, only two XOR operations are involved: 0 XOR 0 = 0 and 1 XOR 1 = 0.
