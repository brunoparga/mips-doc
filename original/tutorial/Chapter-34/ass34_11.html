Traversing a Linked List

## linked.asm --- hard-coded linked list
##
          .text
          .globl main
          
main:
             $s0,elmnt01    # get the address of the first node
          
loop:     beqz   $s0,done       # while not null

          . . .          
done:    

          . . .
                     
          .data

elmnt01:  .word  1
          .word  elmnt02

elmnt02:  .word  2
          .word elmnt03 

          . . .

Here is a program that visits every node of a linked list and prints out its data. A visit to every node of a data structure is called a traversal.

The first statement loads register $s0 with the address of the first node.
