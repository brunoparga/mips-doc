Positional Notation

This is just base 10 positional notation which you know about already. The decimal point is put between the 100 position and the 10-1 position. Here is a longer example:


3 5 7 . 2 8 4
3×102 5×101 7×10 0 . 2×10-1 8×10-2 4×10-3

The notation 357.284 means 3 times 100, plus 5 times 10, plus 7 times 1, plus 2 times 0.1, plus 8 times 0.01, plus 4 times 0.001.
